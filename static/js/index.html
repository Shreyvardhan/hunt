The Mother’s International School Information Network (MINET) is organizing its annual IT symposium, X, from July 31, 2014 to August 1, 2014.

X is an exclusive invite-only event. Invites are extended to no more than 16 of the very best schools in New Delhi, based on significant achievements in the previous years.
